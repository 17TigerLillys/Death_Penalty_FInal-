Home Page

The intent of this project and this site was NOT to try to convince someone whether execution is just or that the Death Penalty should be widely adopted or abolished. Instead, this site provides the viewer an opportunity to interact with data that was mined on the subject.

This site will provide the user an opportunity to interactively look at execution data within the United States over the last several decades, compare executions by race and state as well as view legality and frequency maps by State. In addition, this site will also provide the opportunity to take a look at the concept of "executions" on a global scale.
